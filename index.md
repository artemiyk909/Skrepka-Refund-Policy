# 4. How to Request a Refund

## A. Web Purchases (via Paddle)

If you made a purchase through our website using Paddle, your transaction is subject to Paddle's Terms and Conditions.

To request a refund:

1. Contact Paddle Support directly through their website.
2. Use the support link provided in your Paddle payment receipt.
3. Alternatively, email **[billing@skrepka.app](mailto:billing@skrepka.app)** and include your Paddle Order ID.

If applicable, we may forward your request to Paddle for review.

Paddle reserves the right to deny refund requests that do not comply with this Refund Policy or its own policies.

---

## B. Apple (iOS) Users

To request a refund for a purchase made through Apple:

1. Visit Apple's official refund portal.
2. Sign in using your Apple ID.
3. Locate your Skrepka transaction.
4. Select **"Request a Refund."**
5. Follow Apple's instructions and submit your request.

Apple's refund decisions are final and governed exclusively by Apple's Media Services Terms and Conditions.

---

## C. Google Play (Android) Users

To request a refund for a Google Play purchase:

1. Open Google Play or your Google Play purchase history.
2. Locate the Skrepka transaction.
3. Select **"Request a refund"** or **"Report a problem."**
4. Complete and submit the request form.

Refund decisions are made solely by Google under the Google Play Terms of Service.
