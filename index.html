SKREPKA – REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Thank you for using Skrepka!

This Refund Policy ("Policy") governs all purchases made within the Skrepka mobile application and on our official website, including premium subscriptions and our virtual currency, "Skrepki."

1. General Principle: All Sales Are Final

Except as expressly provided in this Policy, or as required by applicable mandatory consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction, all purchases made through the Service are final, non-refundable, and non-exchangeable.

By purchasing premium subscriptions or virtual currency ("Skrepki"), you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You receive immediate access to the digital content and services.
  • Your right of withdrawal expires the moment the digital content or service is delivered to your account.
  • The Company and its payment partners are under no obligation to provide a refund or credit for any reason, including but not limited to:
    • Account termination;
    • Dissatisfaction with platform interactions;
    • Application downtime;
    • Unused virtual items.

2. Virtual Currency ("Skrepki")

"Skrepki" are strictly digital licenses for use within the app and website. They:

  • Have no cash value.
  • Cannot be refunded or exchanged.
  • Cannot be converted into real-world currency or redeemed for legal tender.
  • Cannot be transferred to another user.

If your account is banned, suspended, or terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service, all remaining "Skrepki" will be permanently forfeited and will not be refunded.

3. Payment Processing and Merchant of Record

Skrepka does not directly process payments or store financial information. All billing, purchases, and subscription renewals are handled exclusively by authorized third-party platforms.

Mobile Purchases

  • Apple App Store (Apple Inc.)
  • Google Play Store (Google LLC)

Web Purchases

Stripe acts as our official payment processor and Merchant of Record for web transactions. Stripe is responsible for:

  • Processing payments
  • Customer billing
  • Tax compliance (including VAT/GST where applicable)
  • Customer support related to web payments

4. How to Request a Refund

A. Web Purchases (via Stripe)

If you made a purchase through our website using Stripe, your transaction is subject to Stripe's Terms and Conditions. To request a refund:

  1. Use the "Manage Subscription" or "Customer Portal" link provided in your Stripe payment receipt.
  2. Alternatively, email sehatsuksesindonesia@gmail.com and include your Stripe receipt number or Order ID. We will review your request in accordance with this Policy.

We reserve the right to deny refund requests that do not comply with this Refund Policy.

B. Apple (iOS) Users

To request a refund for a purchase made through Apple:

  1. Visit Apple's official refund portal at reportaproblem.apple.com.
  2. Sign in using your Apple ID.
  3. Locate your Skrepka transaction.
  4. Select "Request a Refund."

C. Google Play (Android) Users

To request a refund for a Google Play purchase:

  1. Open Google Play or your Google Play purchase history.
  2. Locate the Skrepka transaction.
  3. Select "Request a refund" or "Report a problem."

5. Subscription Cancellations

Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle (for example, monthly) until canceled.

Web Subscriptions (Stripe)

You may cancel your web subscription at any time by:

  • Using the subscription management page within your Skrepka account settings; or
  • Clicking the cancellation link included in any Stripe billing receipt email.

Mobile Subscriptions

To avoid future charges, you must cancel your subscription through your Apple ID subscription settings or Google Play subscription settings at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

Post-Cancellation Access

After cancellation:

  • Premium access remains active until the end of the current paid billing period.
  • No partial refunds will be provided for unused time.
  • Previously purchased "Skrepki" remain subject to the Terms of Service and this Refund Policy.

6. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If you initiate an unauthorized chargeback or payment dispute through your Bank, Credit card issuer, PayPal, or Payment provider for a valid purchase made on Skrepka (including purchases processed by Apple, Google, or Stripe), we reserve the right to immediately suspend or terminate your account without prior notice.

Upon termination, you will permanently forfeit access to premium services and any remaining "Skrepki."

7. Contact Information

For questions or clarification regarding this Refund Policy (excluding direct mobile refund requests, which must be submitted through Apple or Google), please contact:
Email: sehatsuksesindonesia@gmail.com
